Bible Study

Peacemakers in a Divided World

Biblical peace requires courage, truth, humility, and love.

📖 Matthew 5:9
1

Read the Scripture

Read Matthew 5:9 alongside Romans 12:18. Notice that peacemaking is active, not passive.

2

Observation

Peacemakers do not ignore injustice or pretend conflict does not exist. They pursue reconciliation without surrendering truth.

3

Application

Think of one strained relationship. What faithful step toward peace is within your control?

4

Discussion Questions

  • How is peacemaking different from people-pleasing?
  • When is a boundary necessary?
  • How can truth and grace work together?
🙏

Closing Prayer

Lord, help me receive Your truth, live it faithfully, and remember that my deepest identity is found in You. Amen.
