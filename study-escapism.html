Bible Study

Escapism vs. Eternal Hope

Set your mind on Christ without abandoning the work God has given you today.

📖 Colossians 3:1-4
1

Read the Scripture

Read Colossians 3:1-4. Pay attention to what Paul means by setting your mind on things above.

2

Observation

Christian hope is not denial. It gives us courage to face the present because our future is secure in Christ.

3

Application

Name one responsibility you have been avoiding. How can eternal hope help you engage it faithfully instead of escaping it?

4

Discussion Questions

  • What is the difference between rest and avoidance?
  • How can hope make us more present?
  • What practical step can you take this week?
🙏

Closing Prayer

Lord, help me receive Your truth, live it faithfully, and remember that my deepest identity is found in You. Amen.
