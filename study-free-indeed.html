Freedom in Christ is both a gift to receive and a life to practice.

📖 John 8:31-36
1

Read the Scripture

Read John 8:31-36 and notice the connection between abiding in Jesus' teaching and experiencing freedom.

2

Observation

Jesus describes freedom as something deeper than circumstances. Sin enslaves, but the Son gives a new identity and a new way to live.

3

Application

Identify one habit, fear, or false belief that still acts like a master. Bring it honestly before Christ.

4

Discussion Questions

  • What does freedom mean in this passage?
  • Why is truth essential to freedom?
  • How can brothers in Christ support one another?
🙏

Closing Prayer

Lord, help me receive Your truth, live it faithfully, and remember that my deepest identity is found in You. Amen.
