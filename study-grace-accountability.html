Bible Study

Grace and Accountability

Grace does not erase accountability. Accountability does not erase dignity.

📖 John 1:14
1

Read the Scripture

Read John 1:14 and Galatians 6:1-2. Notice how truth, grace, restoration, and humility belong together.

2

Observation

Jesus is full of grace and truth. Christian accountability should reflect both, confronting what harms while protecting the dignity of people.

3

Application

Where do you tend to lean too far: avoiding truth or withholding grace? Ask God for balance.

4

Discussion Questions

  • Why do people confuse grace with permission?
  • How can accountability become harmful?
  • What does restoration look like?
🙏

Closing Prayer

Lord, help me receive Your truth, live it faithfully, and remember that my deepest identity is found in You. Amen.
