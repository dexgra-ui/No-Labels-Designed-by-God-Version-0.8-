Our Ministry

We are a family seeking to serve Christ together by creating biblical resources that help people discover who they are in Him.

Why we exist

Labels describe. God defines.

People are often reduced to their mistakes, backgrounds, struggles, politics, health, age, or other people’s expectations. We believe every person bears the image of God and should encounter biblical truth with dignity, clarity, grace, and hope.

No Labels, Designed by God exists to point people beyond the names the world gives them and toward the identity, purpose, and new life found in Jesus Christ.

“We want to build something that points people to Jesus and can be carried by future generations.”
