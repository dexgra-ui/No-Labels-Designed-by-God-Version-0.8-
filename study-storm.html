Bible Study

Faith in the Storm

Jesus may not prevent every storm, but His presence changes how we face it.

📖 Mark 4:35-41
1

Read the Scripture

Read Mark 4:35-41. Notice what the disciples see, what they fear, and what Jesus reveals.

2

Observation

The storm exposes the disciples' fear, but it also reveals Jesus' authority. Their greatest lesson is not about weather. It is about who is in the boat.

3

Application

What storm are you facing? Write one honest fear and one truth about Jesus that speaks to it.

4

Discussion Questions

  • Why did the disciples assume Jesus did not care?
  • How does fear distort our view of God?
  • What does trust look like before the storm ends?
🙏

Closing Prayer

Lord, help me receive Your truth, live it faithfully, and remember that my deepest identity is found in You. Amen.
