Bible Study

Learning to See Yourself Through God's Eyes

Grace changes more than your destination. It changes how you see yourself.

📖 Ephesians 2:1-10
1

Read the Scripture

Read Ephesians 2:1-10. Notice the movement from death to life, from distance to belonging, and from emptiness to purpose.

2

Observation

Paul says salvation is a gift of grace, not a reward for performance. Our good works do not create our worth. They flow from what God has already done.

3

Application

What label have you allowed to become louder than God's truth? Write it down, then replace it with one truth from this passage.

4

Discussion Questions

  • Why do people struggle to receive grace?
  • How does verse 10 reshape the way you view purpose?
  • What would change if you truly believed you are God's workmanship?
🙏

Closing Prayer

Lord, help me receive Your truth, live it faithfully, and remember that my deepest identity is found in You. Amen.
